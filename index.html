Installations

Whether you need new faucets, sinks, toilets, or a complete plumbing system for a renovation or new construction, we’ve got all your plumbing needs covered. Our experienced plumbers ensure every installation is done with precision and care, adhering to the highest industry standards. We also install sewer systems, tankless water heaters, sump pumps, and water filtration systems, ensuring everything works perfectly from day one. Our goal is to provide hassle-free, efficient installations that exceed your expectations.

Hot Water Heaters

Never face a cold shower again; we service all types of water heaters, including tankless, electric, and gas models. We're proud to be Navien Tankless Service Specialists and work with all kinds of tankless models. Common issues we address include inconsistent water temperatures, strange noises, leaks, and complete system failures. With a quick service call, one of our skilled technicians can quickly diagnose the problem and provide hot water heater repairs to restore hot water tanks and supply. Whether it's a minor fix or a major repair, we’re committed to delivering the best service and ensuring your home has a steady supply of hot water.

Water Main Replacement

Every home or business should have a reliable and clean water supply. That's why it can be a serious issue when your main water line breaks. Whether you’re experiencing low water pressure, frequent leaks, or discoloured water, our team is equipped to handle the replacement with minimal disruption. We always provide great service and use quality materials to replace old or damaged water mains efficiently. Our process includes a thorough inspection, precise excavation, and careful installation.

Common Plumbing Problems

Both homes and businesses can face a variety of plumbing issues that professional plumbing companies need to address.

Common plumbing problems include leaky faucets, clogged drains, and running toilets, leading to higher water bills and potential water damage to your property. Burst pipes, water heater failures, and low water pressure are frequent concerns that can disrupt daily living or business activities.

Our team is well-equipped to handle all types of plumbing issues, including severe problems like sewer line blockages and gas line issues, which can pose serious risks and require immediate attention.

We continually assess the problem with a thorough inspection (and, if necessary, a video camera pipe inspection), followed by a detailed, comprehensive solution, which can include drain cleaning, pipe repair, water heater service, and emergency plumbing repairs, ensuring your plumbing system operates smoothly and efficiently.
